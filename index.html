Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. here is recipe for deliciously rich Scrambled Eggs.

Ingredient

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbs butter
  • 3 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter is a frying pan
  2. Gently mix the egg and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once butter has melted add egg and cream.
  4. Using a spatula fold for making this food.(this would takes 20 minute.)
  5. When the egg are still moist remove from the heat.(it will continue to cook on the plate until served.)
